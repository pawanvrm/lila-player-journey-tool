# Insights

## Player Movement Patterns
Players tend to cluster around central areas of the map.

## Hotspots
Heatmap shows high activity zones where most interactions happen.

## Low Activity Areas
Some regions are rarely visited, indicating possible design gaps.

## Player Behavior
Some players follow predictable routes, while others explore more dynamically.

## Map Design Implications
- High-traffic areas may need balancing
- Low-traffic areas may need incentives (loot, objectives)

## Value for Level Designers
This tool helps designers:
- Understand player flow
- Identify hotspots and dead zones
- Improve map balance and engagement
